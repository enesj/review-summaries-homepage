üîπ Supported Languages

The official language of this extension is English, but our summary generation fully supports the following languages: Bosnian, Croatian, Czech, Danish, Dutch, French, German, Italian, Macedonian, Polish, Portuguese, Serbian, Slovenian, Spanish, and Turkish.

For optimal performance and quality, the summary generation works best with English, French, Italian, and Spanish. Summaries in these languages are also generated faster and cheaper.

The summary language is determined by the language setting in your Google Search Settings.

By changing the language setting, you can generate summaries in your preferred language from the list of supported languages.
